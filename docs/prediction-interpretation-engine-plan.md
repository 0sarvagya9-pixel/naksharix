# Prediction Interpretation Engine Plan

## Current Status

The interpretation engine is schema-only and blocked. No public personalized prediction engine is active.

## Future Rule Shape

Rules should include:

- Scope: graha, rashi, bhava, nakshatra, dasha, transit, or varga.
- Required verified inputs.
- Conditions JSON.
- Confidence level.
- Language.
- Claim boundary.
- Editorial review status.

## Activation Gate

Activate only after verified chart data, reviewed rule content, multilingual QA, and safety review are complete.

## Claim Boundary

Interpretations must be reflective and non-fatalistic. They must not guarantee job, marriage, wealth, health, or legal outcomes.
